Faith Is the Title Deed to Our Joint-Heir Ownershipr

Faith get’s hold of information that cannot be gained, perceived, or experienced by the five physical senses—taste, smell, feel, touch, or hearing with the natural ear. In other words, faith and revelation are divinely joined.Pure, tested, and true faith is the title deed to every aspect and item of the inheritance we have in Jesus—the benefits of our gospel partnership with him.
